[BeeHive](https://tools.abeeway.io/beehive) is a Chrome application that can be used to import and export configuration files and to connect to the device Command Line Interface over Bluetooth or USB.

## Connecting over Bluetooth

1. Pair your computer with the device using the UI of your operating system (Windows, Linux).
2. Open the [BeeHive](https://tools.abeeway.io/beehive) App in your Chrome browser.
3. Click the *"Connect a BLE Device"* button and wait until your device name, starting with the *"AB"* prefix, appears on the screen.
4. Select the device and click on *"Pair"*.

## Connecting over USB

1. Open the casing of the device.
2. Remove the PCB from the device.
3. Find the USB port on the PCB and connect it to your computer with a USB cable.
4. Open the [BeeHive](https://tools.abeeway.io/beehive) App in your Chrome browser.
5. Click the *"Connect a USB Device"* button and wait until the device serial port appears on the screen.
6. Select the port and click on *"Connect"*.

## Using BeeHive

After the device is connected, you can use the controls at the top of the screen to import or export configuration files, or to open the CLI interface.
